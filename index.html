Why Understanding Graph Traversal is Challenging?

Many students struggle to grasp graph traversal concepts like BFS and DFS. The abstract nature of nodes, edges, and algorithms can be overwhelming. This visualizer aims to simplify these concepts by providing interactive, step-by-step explanations.

Why this Visualizer?

Understanding graph algorithms can be tricky, but what if you could see each step unfold right in front of your eyes? This interactive visualizer helps you learn complex concepts like BFS and DFS by watching them in action.

Our goal is to simplify abstract ideas by providing a hands-on experience. With step-by-step visual explanations, learners can clearly see how nodes are visited, making learning engaging, visual, and effective.
